Sponsoring NixCon 2017

NixCon 2017 is a volunteer and community conference from and for NixOS contributors that depends on your sponsorships to happen.

We offer Gold, Silver, Lanyrd and Contributor sponsor packages. If you have other ideas how you want to sponsor the event, please talk to us. We are happy to create a new sponsor package just for you.

Silver Sponsor ‐ 250 € (excl. VAT)

Benefits:

  • Logo on nixcon2017.org website
  • 1 included tickets

Gold Sponsor ‐ 1000 € (excl. VAT)

Benefits:

  • Logo on nixcon2017.org website
  • 50 word description on the website
  • 4 included tickets
  • Flyer/swag on shared table

Lanyrd Sponsor ‐ 400 € (excl. VAT)

You sponsor the lanyard for all attendees. We will use any lanyard you provide. We need around 140 lanyards from you to make sure everybody gets one.


Benefits:

  • Logo on nixcon2017.org website
  • 2 included tickets
  • Flyer/swag on shared table

Contributor Sponsor ‐ 1000 € (excl. VAT)

We aim to help people who make significant contributions to Nix and can't afford travelling to attend.


Benefits:

  • Logo on nixcon2017.org website
  • 50 word description on the website
  • 4 included tickets
  • Flyer/swag on shared table
